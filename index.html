GuessDue

Family and friends trade diapers and baby supplies to guess about your child's birth.

GuessDue will be an interactive website where your family and friends can exchange baby products for guesses about your soon to be bundle of joy. Expecting families can choose and customize what things will be guessed about. They can also set up custom prizes for the correct guessers.

Family and friends can try and guess things such as:

  • baby's birth date, hour, minute
  • baby's gender
  • birth weight
  • eye color
  • hair color
  • and other statstics...

Guessers can obtain additional guesses by purchasing items to help the family once baby arrives.

The expecting family determines the number of guesses per item and creates their custom list of items that are eligible for guesses.

Some of the items available to be exchanged for guesses are:

  • Diapers
  • Bottles
  • Clothing
  • Toys

Guessing codes can be created and passed out around the baby shower for people who opted to give in person rather than online.

Reward the correct guessers with cash prizes or gift cards. You get to choose the type of reward and the number of winners that can be awarded.

Once you enter the final statistics of the baby, the correct guessers are automatically chosen and notified of their success. Results get sent out to all of the participants and can be enhanced to include newborn pictures or additional information you would like to share.

Only after the contest has ended are guessers billed for the items they traded guesses for. Adjustments can be made to the item's sizes or colors before being automatically shipped to your home.

You have a newborn to keep track of so we'll automatically ship out gift cards or cash prizes to the correct guessers.
